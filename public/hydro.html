Hydro Energy

Hydro energy, also known as hydropower, is a renewable energy source that harnesses the power of flowing water to generate electricity. It is one of the oldest and most widely used forms of renewable energy, with hydroelectric plants found all over the world. Hydroelectricity is produced by directing water through turbines, which spin generators to produce electricity. It is a clean, reliable, and cost-effective source of energy that can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and combat climate change.
