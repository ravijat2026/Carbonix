Solar Energy

Solar energy is renewable, clean, and abundant energy derived from the sun's radiation. It's harnessed through technologies like solar panels and can be used for electricity generation, heating, and transportation. It's environmentally friendly, cost-effective, versatile, and contributes to sustainable development by reducing emissions and promoting energy access.
